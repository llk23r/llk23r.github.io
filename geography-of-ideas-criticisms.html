The Geography of Ideas

Criticisms by LLM

Survivorship Bias

Not all deep work gets found—some ideas remain undiscovered due to missing urgency or loss.

Depth Requirement Ambiguity

How can creators reliably judge if their work has enough depth before it’s found?

Social and Structural Barriers

Barriers such as geography, language, or institutional bias can prevent discovery regardless of depth.

Network Effects and Path Dependence

The knowledge space evolves—new discoveries change what is possible or visible.

Urgency Versus Fads

Not all urgent searches reflect enduring needs—some are fleeting trends.
